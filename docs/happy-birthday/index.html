Happy Birthday

Meike’s birthday is coming. Since we are still stuck at home and cannot celebrate together, I would like to make a collective happy birthday video for Meike.

This will take you about 5-10 minutes.

This page will guide you step-by-step how to record a synchronised video of the happy birthday song. I will collect these videos, make a video collage and share it with Meike on her birthday.

Step 1: Prepare

We will be singing “Happy Birthday to you” song. This website has a video recorder with the lyrics with the melody. Please follow the timing of the melody as closely as you can.

On Computer with a webcam

You will need to download the Google Chrome browser, because other browsers have slightly different timing - this might make you out-of-sync from other singers. Then just open this webpage.

On iOS or Android

You will need to download the Google Chrome app. It is pre-installed on most Android phones. You can record in portrait or landscape.

Important: it seems not to work on iOS!

Environment

The videos come out better if they are recorded in a well lit room with little noise.

Configure (advanced)

For me default settings worked well, but if you have multiple cameras or mics, you might want to change the defaults in the options below.

Step 4: Send me the video

Download the video by clicking the “Download” button above (it might be in a strange format, but it does work). Then send me this video via email (k.misiunas@gmail.com) or WhatsApp (+447594321901). If the file is over 20MB, you might need to upload it to Google Drive or Dropbox and share the link.

Step 5 (optional): “Hello Meike from X”

I will add short videos with various greetings (each about 5-10 seconds), before the collective song. For example, you could say “Hi Meike from Essen” or “I wish you many cupcakes this year!” or anything else. Can be in English or German.

If you can, please send me such a video as well. Feel free to record it with an interesting background: maybe the city you live in, a hospital, any place that Meike would recognise.

Important: Please make sure to record in landscape (wide image).

Data privacy

All the recording above is done in your browser and the video is NOT sent anywhere. You need to download it and send it to me. I will store this data on my personal laptop for the purpose of making a happy birthday video for Meike. The final video will be uploaded to private YouTube, where only people with a secret link can see the video.
