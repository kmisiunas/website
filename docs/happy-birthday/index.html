Happy Birthday

Meike’s birthday is coming soon! Since we are still stuck at home and cannot celebrate together, I would like to make a collective happy birthday video for Meike.

This page will guide you step-by-stem how to record synchronised video of the classic happy birthday song. I will collect these videos, make a video collage and share it with Meike on her birthday.

Step 1: Prepare

We will be singing “Happy Birthday to you” song. To make sure we are all synchronised in the final video, I added the lyrics with the melody. Try following them as closely as you can.

On Computer with webcam

You will need to download Google Chrome browser.

On iOS or Android

You will need to download Google Chrome browser app. It is pre-installed on most Android phones.

Important: on iOS, by default Google Chrome does not have access to the camera. Go to Settings > Chrome > Turn camera on.

Configure (advanced)

For me default settings worked well, but if you have multiple cameras or mics, you might want to change the defaults in the options below.
